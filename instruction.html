Game Play Instruction

  1. Turn on the volume!!
  2. Click "Start" to start the game with basic ice breaker questions!
  3. Finish the answering the question, that leads you to the summary page
  4. Change category by clicking on 'Trivia Category' in the navigation menu.
  5. Press start to play the game!
  6. Have fun and hope you like it!


Getting annoyed by background music? Turn it off by clicking "Audio" on the top right corner in navigation menu.
Click on "Game" in the top left corner in navigation menu to play!
